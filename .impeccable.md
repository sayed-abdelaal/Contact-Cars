## Design Context

### Users
People shopping for a car in Egypt who are comparing instalment-friendly options on Contact Cars. They are often researching on mobile or laptop while balancing budget, family needs, and confidence in the numbers shown to them.

### Brand Personality
Trustworthy, clear, modern. The interface should feel stable, informative, and financially responsible rather than flashy, gimmicky, or overly startup-like.

### Aesthetic Direction
Light-mode first, clean automotive-finance UI with strong hierarchy, crisp surfaces, and confident calls to action. It should feel like a credible marketplace and finance guide, not a speculative fintech dashboard or a loud promo site.

### Design Principles
1. Make financial choices feel understandable and low-friction.
2. Prioritize trust signals, legibility, and calm clarity over decoration.
3. Use shared systems so feature experiments stay consistent across iterations.
4. Keep interactive states obvious, accessible, and predictable.
5. Let emphasis come from hierarchy and precision, not visual noise.
